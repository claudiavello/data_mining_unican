Comments about both polts:

  • Many points are located near the top of the plot, with confidence values close to 1. These rules are very reliable for predicting that a mushroom is edible or poisonous when the LHS conditions are met.

  • Most rules have a support between 0.1 and 0.3, with a few reaching higher support levels, up to around 0.5. Higher support rules are more general and cover a significant portion of the dataset.

  • Darker points, representing rules with a lift greater than 1.5, are generally located near the top of the plot. These rules are both reliable (high confidence) and significant (high lift). However, they often have moderate support, meaning they may not be the most frequent but are still highly interesting.

  • In the lower left of the plot, there are points with low support (around 0.1) and confidence below 0.6. These rules are less frequent and less reliable, making them less relevant for predicting edible mushrooms.
